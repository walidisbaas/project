ABOUT ARTIGIN

Artigin is an online research platform that brings together all the information you need to find out more about the artwork you own. Get in touch with Rijksmuseum, do your own research, or get in touch with others. All in one place.

Contact form

Use the contact form above to get in touch with the Rijksmuseum Department of Research Services. Easily fill out your contact information, the necessary information about your artwork and specify what you would like to know about the piece you own.

Search guide

Use the search guide to find out about possible ways to do your own research.

Taxation guide

Use the taxation guide to learn about possible ways to get to find out the estimated value of your artpiece.

Community

Get in touch with other art-lovers through the forum. Share your personal experiences or read about others.
